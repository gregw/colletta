Colletta has a terrace converted to a 20m outdoor swimming pool. Above and below the pool are grassed terraces with deck chairs where you can soak up the sun and the views. There are also olive, fig and cherry trees for shade.

The pool is open and supervised from May 15th to September 15th according to the following schedule: 

* 15/5 to 14/7 from 10:00 to 12:00 and from 14:00 to 18:00, 6 days per week (closed Mondays)$$
* 15/7 to 31/7 from 10:00 to 12:00 and from 14:00 to 18:00, 7 days per week$$
*   1/8 to 31/8 from 10:00 to 18:00, 7 days per week$$
*   1/9 to 15/9 from 10:00 to 12:00 and from 14:00 to 18:00, 6 days per week (closed Mondays)$$

Entry to the pool area outside these dates and hours is unauthorised.

There are also toilets, showers and a changing room by the pool, as well as a sauna.
