There are 30 km of pistes composed of 6 easy runs, 2 intermediate and 2 advanced. There are 5 lifts, of which 4 are drag lifts, and one is a double chair with a lift capacity of 1200 people/hr. Snowboards may use any of the pistes along with skiers, although one piste is reserved for instruction. 

Services include:
* ski school
* child minding
* ski hire
* bar and self-service restaurant
* mountain hut with solarium

For more information, consult [[http://www.garessio2000.it/:Garresio 2000]]
