[[/village/gallery/olivefesta04/:Foti da Festa]]

Thanks to the love of the area and the initiative of Ole Wiig, an
architect from Oslo and new apartment owner at Colletta di Castelbianco
and active member of the cultural association, Colletta di Castelbianco
will be holding a 3 day Festival of extra-virgin Olive Oil produced
from Colletta's own taggiasca olives.

Everyone who is interested is invited to actively participate in the harvest of the olives,
as well as their transport to Frantoio Garello in Nasino where they will be pressed and
bottled into the oil, baptized "Goccia Dorata" by Ole.

It is a tribute to the village, a tribute to the environment around it and
to the importance of regional characteristics, a tribute to everyone who
has believed in the revitalization of the village and its continued
improvement and advancement.

It will be an event with a multinational flavour, due to the involvement
of many of the owners of the village, who come from many areas of Europe and
further abroad, and who have indicated that they'll be actively participating.

It is also a symbolic undertaking, in support of local products and the life that revolves around them.
