A beautiful south facing two bedroom apartment with a full kitchen, a garden and a sun terrace. All the windows receive sunshine and have excellent views.

There is a large double bed and lots of storage in the main bedroom. The second bedroom can be configured with a double bed, two singles or as a study/office and has its own entrance. The sofa with its ample chaise longue also converts into a comfortable double bed. There are two complete bathrooms with a shower upstairs and a bath/shower in the other.

Appliances include air conditioner, floor heating, dishwasher, oven/microwave, washer/dryer, DVD, LCD TV, Sky Digital pay TV, broadband Internet connection, WiFi hub and printer. 3 or 4 can eat around the breakfast bar and the designer coffee table easily converts into a dinner table for 8.

Outdoors on the terrace and in the garden, there are tables and chairs for al fresco dining with stunning views. The well maintained garden and lawn includes flowerbeds, kitchen herbs, climbing roses and a shady olive tree. The apartment has its own Olive grove 100 m down  the hill with grassy terraces and dry stone walls in the shade of ancient trees.
