This is a lovely and intimate studio flat that has been finished to the highest standard. It is situated behind the small piazza and not far from the Bar/Osteria.  

The apartment has marble floors throughout and offers a separate studio room with pull-down double  bed and table and chairs, plus a small wardrobe and storage space for clothes. This room also features a TV and an Internet connection. There is a small kitchenette with a sink and two hot plates. The good-sized bathroom is complete with a bath and hand held shower.  There is a small terrace next to the apartment that offers fabulous views over the Pennavaire valley. There is a table and 2 chairs plus a sun lounger - the perfect place to relax in the evening and enjoy a glass of wine.

Lavanda is mostly on one level though there are a few steps  up to the kitchen/bathroom area.
