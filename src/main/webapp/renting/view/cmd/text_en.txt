Galleria is a lovely and spacious 2-bedroom apartment that offers convenience as well as views of the beautiful Pennavaire Mountains. Located in the heart of the village it provides easy access to the piazza, the Osteria/bar, the laundry room and the pool (seasonal). Underfloor heating in all rooms provides comfort for stays in autumn, winter and spring.

It is comprised of a master bedroom, a 2nd bedroom (with twin beds), two bathrooms and a sizeable living, dining and kitchen area plus a lovely terrace. Upon entering the apartment, you are in a large, open room that includes a nicely furnished living area with a pullout couch for additional guest sleeping. The open plan kitchen offers all the amenities you will need during your stay inclusive of a large fridge and a dishwasher.  The eat-in dining area can accommodate up to 6 people and also features attractive shelving set into the surrounding walls.  Just a few steps up from the main room you will find both bedrooms and bathrooms. The bedrooms are good-sized and feature lovely, natural arched ceilings. The twin bedroom has an en suite bath complete with a shower.  The second bathroom is located off the main room (across from the master bedroom).  This bathroom has a bathtub with a shower.

The south-facing terrace just off the living area is perfect for dining al fresco or to watch the sunset. The terrace is well equipped with patio furniture, an umbrella and deck chairs to help you relax after a day's hike.  Additionally there is a good-sized barbeque for your use.

The Galleria apartment can accommodate up to 6 people and is ideal for a family or a group of friends. While this apartment is virtually on one level it should be noted that there are a few steps up to the front door as well as a few steps up to the terrace.
