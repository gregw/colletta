This is a super, newly fitted studio apartment which will sleep up to 5 people. It is situated along a terrace of new studio apartments with stunning views over the village and surrounding hills..

The apartment includes a a double bedroom at the back separated from the living area by a curtain. There is a small kitchen with fridge and hob plus  a dining area with table and 4 chairs and a living room with a pullout couch and a single sofa bed, plus a large wardrobe for storage. The shower room is huge and consists of a roomy shower, basin, WC and bidet. 

Outside there is lovely grassy terrace looking out over the mountains with a table and 4 chairs for outside dining. Privacy is protected by hedges on each side.
