This is a good sized 1-bedroom apartment that can accommodate up to 6 people.  It is conveniently located with the entrance not far from the centre of the village (near the laundry and the Osteria/bar) while the back garden offers access to the swimming pool (seasonal).  There is underfloor heating throughout to provide comfort for stays in autumn, winter and spring. 

This apartment is comprised of 1 bedroom, 1 bathroom, a kitchen and living room.  The kitchen is large and is outfitted with all the amenities you will need during your stay, inclusive of a large refrigerator, a separate freezer and an eat-in dining area.  The living room has a pullout sofa bed as well as a large, round sofa/bed that can provide sleeping for additional guests.  There is a flat-screen TV and a satellite decoder for your entertainment. The master bedroom is a good size and across the hall you will find the bathroom with a tub (shower handle). 

This apartment offers a nicely furnished garden that is accessible from the kitchen as well as the master bedroom.  There is patio furniture, a large umbrella and a built in barbeque for your outdoor dining needs.  It is an ideal place to relax and enjoy the stunning mountain views. 

Ciliegio is best suited for a couple, a family of 4-6 or a group of friends.  It should be noted that there are a few steps internally and externally for this apartment.
