Castagno is a very cozy apartment located in the very heart of Colletta. 
It is made of a spacious main bedroom with a big wardrobe, a second bedroom with a bunkbed, two living rooms ( in one of the which there is a double sofa bed), bathroom ( provided with shower and washing machine) and a luminous,  fully equipped kitchen with dishwasher. 
The apartment, this way, allows for total comfort and indipendence during your holidays.
A wonderful terrace facing both the valley and the main square of the village will be the ideal location for your outdoor meals, accompained by a breathtaking view.  You will also be able to enjoy the evenings of live music offered by the bar directly from your location. 
For your winter stays, Castagno offers underfloor heating.
